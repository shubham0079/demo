Water Jug Problem

Information

The Water Jug Problem is a classic puzzle where you are given two jugs, one of capacity X liters and the other of capacity Y liters. The objective is to measure exactly Z liters of water using these two jugs.

Steps to Solve :-

  1. Fill one of the jugs completely.
  2. Pour the water from the full jug into the other jug until the second jug is full or the first jug is empty.
  3. If the second jug is not full yet, repeat step 2.
  4. If the second jug is full, but there is still remaining water in the first jug, empty the second jug.
  5. Repeat steps 1-4 until you measure exactly Z liters of water.

Algorithm :-

          1. Fill jug A.
          2. While jug B is not full:
              a. Pour as much water as possible from jug A to jug B.
              b. If jug A is empty, fill it.
              c. If jug B is full, empty it.
          3. Repeat steps 2 until the desired amount is reached in jug B.
